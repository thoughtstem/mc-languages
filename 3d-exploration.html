#lang 3d-exploration

About this Language

Use this language to create your own 3D worlds to explore!

  • Customize environments with presets, dressing, colors and more!
  • Add completely customizable 3D objects!
  • Add particle generators; like dust, rain or flying dragons!
  • Animate shapes and 3D models in your world!
To Install

Package Source: https://github.com/thoughtstem/TS-VR-Languages.git?path=3d-exploration

Animations 1

Code a scene with a random-colored cylinder that continuously grows and shrinks.

(define (my-cylinder)
 (basic-cylinder
  #:color (random-color)
  #:animations-list (do-many
                     (animate-scale #:to 5))))

(exploration-scene
#:ground-objects (list
                  (my-cylinder)))

#:sky-objects (list
               (sun)))
